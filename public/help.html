Help

Trucker's informations

  • Trucker name

    • This is the name of the truck, if you don't know, call the driver and he will tell you!

  • Price by kms

    • This is the price of the kilometers. He can change with the trucker, or the driver. If you don't know, call the driver and he will tell you!

  • Price by cubic

    • This is the price of the volume (in m3). He can change with the trucker, or the driver. If you don't know, call the driver and he will tell you!

Shipper's informations

  • Shipper name

    • This is the name of the shipper.

  • Distance

    • This is the distance traveled during the delivery. If you don't know the distance, go to google maps . You need to get the start and the end of the delivery !

  • Volume

    • This is the volume of the delivery. If you don't know, we can't help you.. Sorry !

  • Deductible option

    • In case of an accident/theft, convargo applies a 1000€ deductible. The shipper can reduce the deductible amount from 1000€ to 200€ with a deductible option for a few more euros per volume. The driver is charged an additional 1€/m3 when he chooses the deductible reduction option.
